About Me

I'm passionate about unraveling the mysteries of the Universe. My journey in astrophysics and cosmology is driven by an insatiable curiosity about the cosmos and its intricate workings.

Through my research, I aim to contribute to our understanding of the Universe's structure, evolution, and fundamental laws.

Research Interests

  • Cosmic Voids: Exploring the vast, empty regions of space
  • Variable Stars: Leveraging stellar variability for cosmic distance measurement
  • Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation: Probing the early Universe

My approach combines computational methods with theoretical physics, allowing me to tackle complex problems in astrophysics and cosmology.
