About Me

I am a passionate researcher studying exoplanets, star-planet interaction, and stellar evolution. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Astronomy & Astrophysics at Yale University.

Previously, I completed my BS-MS in Physics from IISER Kolkata and MS thesis project at IISc Bangalore.

My Research

My research focuses on various aspects of exoplanets and stellar astrophysics, including:

  • Exoplanet detection and characterization
  • Star-planet interactions and their impact on planetary systems
  • Stellar evolution and its connection to planetary systems

I utilize a range of techniques such as high-performance computing, machine learning, and statistical analysis to tackle these research problems.

Publications

I have published several papers in peer-reviewed journals, including:

  2. VaTEST II: Statistical Validation of 11 TESS-Detected Exoplanets Orbiting K-type Stars (2023)
