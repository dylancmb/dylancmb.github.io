About Me

I am a crazy guy with a deep love for studying the universe. Currently, I am a 5th year BS-MS student at Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Kolkata, majoring in Physics. I am doing my final year Master's thesis project at Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore.

I intend to continue my research in the field of exoplanets, star-planet interaction, and stellar evolution.

My Research

My research focuses on various aspects of exoplanets and stellar astrophysics, including:

  • Exoplanet detection and characterization
  • Star-planet interactions and their impact on planetary systems
  • Stellar evolution and its connection to planetary systems

I utilize a range of techniques such as high-performance computing, machine learning, and statistical analysis to tackle these research problems.
